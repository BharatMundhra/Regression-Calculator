MEAN VALUE CALCULATION

The mean, most commonly known as the average of a set of numerical values, is a measure of central tendency, a value that estimates the center of a set of numbers.

This calculator uses the following formula to calculate the mean:

SUM (xi) / n

where n is the total number of values and xi (x2, x1, ... ,xn) are the individual numbers in the data set. In words: It's the sum of all values, divided by the total number of values.

To calculate the mean, enter the numerical values in the box above. You may separate individual values by commas. You may also copy and paste data into the text box. Press the "Calculate" button to perform the computation. The mean will be displayed if the calculation is successful.
